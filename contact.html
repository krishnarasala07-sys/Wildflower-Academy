Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what parents ask us most.

What is the admission process? ▼
Simply contact us, visit the school and fill out the admission form. We keep it simple — no entrance tests, no complicated procedures. Just a friendly chat and you're in!
Is there a registration fee? ▼
Yes, there is a one-time registration fee. Please contact us directly for the current fee structure as it may vary by program. We ensure complete transparency — no hidden charges.
What is the teacher to child ratio? ▼
We maintain a very low ratio — 1 teacher for every 6–8 children in Playgroup and Nursery, and 1 teacher for every 10–12 children in Jr. KG and Sr. KG. This ensures every child gets personal attention.
Do you provide transport? ▼
We are working on arranging safe, GPS-tracked transport for our students. Please contact us to check current availability for your area.
Can parents visit during school hours? ▼
Absolutely! We have an open-door policy. Parents are welcome to visit, observe and stay involved. We believe a strong parent-school partnership is key to a child's happiness and growth.
What if my child cries during the first few days? ▼
This is completely normal and we are very experienced in handling it! Our teachers are trained to comfort and settle new children with patience and love. We also offer a gentle settling-in period so children transition at their own pace.
