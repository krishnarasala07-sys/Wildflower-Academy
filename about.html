Our School

Our Story

Wildflower Academy was born from a simple belief — that every child deserves a warm, joyful and safe place to take their very first steps into learning.

We are a newly founded preschool, and while we may be new, our passion for early childhood education runs deep. Our founder and teachers have spent years working with young children and understanding what truly helps them grow.

We chose the name "Wildflower" because wildflowers grow freely, beautifully and in their own unique way — just like every child in our care.

🌱 Joyful Learning 💛 Child-Centred 🤝 Open to Families 🎨 Creative Expression
🔭

Our Vision

To be a preschool where every child feels seen, valued and excited to learn — a place that families trust and children love.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide a play-based, nurturing environment that supports the whole child — their mind, heart, body and creativity — right from the very beginning.

💎

Our Values

Kindness, curiosity, inclusion and joy. These four values guide everything we do — from how we design our classrooms to how we speak to our children.

What Makes Us Different

We may be new, but these are the promises we make to every family.

01

Every child is known by name

Small batches mean our teachers genuinely know and understand each child personally.

02

Parents are always welcome

Open-door policy — you can visit, observe, and be involved as much as you like.

03

No pressure, no rush

We follow each child's pace. There are no tests, no rankings — just exploration and joy.

04

Daily updates to parents

You'll always know how your child's day went — photos, notes and open communication every day.
